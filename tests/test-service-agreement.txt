2. TERM AND TERMINATION

2.1 Initial Term. This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of three (3) years, unless earlier terminated as provided herein.

2.2 Renewal. This Agreement automatically renews for successive one (1) year terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days before the end of the then-current term.

2.3 Termination for Convenience. Either party may terminate this Agreement (but not individual SOWs) upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

2.4 Termination for Cause. Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party:
   (a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of written notice
   (b) Becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors
   (c) Ceases business operations
   (d) Commits fraud or engages in illegal activities related to this Agreement

2.5 Termination of SOWs. Individual SOWs may be terminated as specified therein, which shall not affect this master Agreement or other SOWs.

2.6 Effect of Termination:
   (a) Provider shall immediately cease work on all SOWs
   (b) Client shall pay for all work completed and expenses incurred through termination date
   (c) Provider shall deliver all work product and Client materials within fifteen (15) days
   (d) Client shall return or destroy all Provider confidential information
   (e) Sections 5 (Intellectual Property), 6 (Confidentiality), 8 (Limitation of Liability), 9 (Indemnification), and 11 (General Provisions) survive termination

2.7 Termination Fees. If Client terminates for convenience:
   - Before 25% completion: 25% of remaining SOW fees
   - Before 50% completion: 50% of remaining SOW fees
   - Before 75% completion: 75% of remaining SOW fees
   - After 75% completion: 100% of remaining SOW fees
